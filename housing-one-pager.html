One-time top-up to the Canada Housing Benefit

Status: Not yet available

The government is proposing a one-time payment to help low-income renters. If legislation passes as drafted, this benefit would be available to renters who:

  • have an adjusted net income for 2021 of less than $35,000 for families, or less than $20,000 for individuals
  • pay at least 30 per cent of their income on rent for their own principal residence in Canada

Applications are not yet available.
