Contact the CRA

Online

Use your CRA My Account to:

  • apply for the One-time top-up to the Canada Housing Benefit
  • update your personal information, including your address and your marital status
  • update your direct deposit information
Sign in to My Account Register

By phone

Call the CRA for more information or help by phone.

What do you want to call about?

  • You have questions about the One-time top-up to the Canada Housing Benefit
  • You have questions about your CRA account information
Before you call

To confirm your identity, you'll need your:

  • social insurance number (SIN)
  • full name and date of birth
  • complete address
  • assessed tax return, notice of assessment or reassessment, other tax document, or be signed in to CRA My Account
If you're calling on behalf of someone else

If you are the friend or family member of someone who needs assistance applying over the phone and you are calling on their behalf, they must be present on the call to give their consent.

You will also be asked to verify your own identity when you call.
