Read the Privacy Statement

When you apply for the one-time Canada Housing Benefit payment, your personal information is collected under the authority of the Canada Emergency Student Benefit Act, which is being jointly administered by Employment and Social Development Canada (ESDC) and the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA). Your personal information will be used for the purpose of (i) administering your application, and could be used for (ii) issuing a payment to you, (iii) verifying that you meet the program's eligibility requirements, and to (iv) collect any overpayments or erroneous payments that may have been issued to you. Failure to provide information will affect your consideration for benefits.

Please note that to process your application we will need to access information in your tax file. Your information may be shared with ESDC for verification and reporting purposes. Under the Privacy Act, you have a right of protection, access to and correction of your personal information and to file a complaint with the Privacy Commissioner of Canada regarding our handling of your information. Personal information banks for this program is under development and will be available in Info Source.

With My Account

Processing times: Payments for applications through My Account will take between 5 to 10 days to process.

Sign in to your CRA My Account Register

Hours of service for CRA My Account

If you don't have or can't sign in to My Account with CRA

You have two options to get access to an account where you can apply without waiting or calling:

  • Switch over to My Account from a My Service Canada Account: Option 1 of 2

    If you have an online account with Service Canada, you can sign in with them, and switch over to a CRA My Account to access the application.

    1. Sign in to your My Service Canada Account
    2. Switch over to a CRA My Account with the "button label" link in the bottom right of the main account landing page
    3. Apply for the one-time Canada Housing Benefit payment through the application linked in the blue alert on the My Account landing page.

  • Register for My Account and apply without waiting: Option 1 of 2

    You can register for CRA My Account and apply for the one-time Canada Housing Benefit payment right away, without waiting for a security code.

    1. Register for My Account to get limited access right away
    2. Go to the Canada Housing Benefit application from the alert at the top of the limited access My Account page once you're registered
    3. Apply for the benefit through the My Account with limited access
    4. After you apply, CRA will send you a security code to finish setting up your full access My Account later

if you are unable to sign in to or register for a CRA My Account, you can apply online through the temporary application form application.

Verifying your eligibility

The Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) will verify your eligibility to receive the one-time payment after you have applied. You or your landlord may receive a notice asking you to contact us to validate your application. This is needed to verify your information and process your application.

In cases where claimants have received a payment and are later found to be ineligible, they will be contacted to make arrangements to repay any applicable amounts. Canadians can also report suspected Canada Housing Payment misuse through the CRA’s Leads program.
