How to apply

On this page

  • Application deadline
  • Apply online or by phone

Application deadline

If you are eligible, applications are currently open.

The last day you can apply is Friday, March 31, 2023 (11:59 pm ET).

Apply online or by phone

The easiest way to apply for the One-time top-up to the Canada Housing Benefit is online through CRA My Account. Make sure you're ready to apply.

Read the Privacy Statement

Personal information (including the SIN) is collected under the authority of the Rental Housing Benefit Act and will be used to administer and enforce this act, including compliance activities. The Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) will use the personal information you provide to us, including information accessed from your tax file, for the purposes (i) administering your application, and could be used for (ii) issuing a payment to you, (iii) verifying that you meet the program's eligibility requirements, and to (iv) collect any overpayments or erroneous payments that may have been issued to you. Your personal information may also be disclosed to other federal, provincial, territorial or aboriginal government institutions to the extent authorized by law. Failure to provide information will affect your consideration for benefits.

Personal information will be described in personal information bank (under development) and published on canada.ca/cra-information-about-programs. Under the Privacy Act, individuals have a right of protection, access to and correction or notation of their personal information and to file a complaint with the Privacy Commissioner of Canada regarding the Canada Revenue Agency’s handling of their information.

With My Account

Payment processing times are 5 to 10 days

Sign in to your CRA My Account Register

Hours of service for CRA My Account

If you don't have or can't sign in to My Account with CRA

You have two options to apply without waiting or calling:

  • Access CRA My Account from My Service Canada Account

    You can sign in to My Service Canada Account to access CRA My Account.

    In your My Service Canada Account:

    1. Click on "Access CRA"
    2. Select "I agree" on the Registration and Authentication page

    This transfers you to CRA My Account.

  • Register for My Account and apply without waiting: Option 1 of 2

    You can register for CRA My Account and apply for the One-time top-up to the Canada Housing Benefit right away, without waiting for a security code.

    1. Register for My Account to get limited service right away
    2. Apply for the one-time Canada Housing Benefit payment
With the alternative application form

Payment processing times are 30 to 60 days

If you cannot apply with My Account

If you are unable to sign in to or register for a CRA My Account, you can apply online through the alternative application form.

By phone
Contact the CRA to apply by phone or TTY

Payment processing times are 5 to 10 days

Before you call

To verify your identity, you'll need your:

  • social insurance number (SIN)
  • full name and date of birth
  • complete address
  • assessed tax return, notice of assessment or reassessment, other tax document, or be signed in to CRA My Account
If you're calling on behalf of someone else

If you are the friend or family member of someone who needs assistance applying over the phone and you are calling on their behalf, they must be present on the call to give their consent.

You will also be asked to verify your own identity when you call.
